We're Hiring

Shooting Creek Vines is now seeking applications for tasting room staff and vineyard technician. All applicants must be over 21.

Tasting room personnel must be enthusiastic, friendly and capable of sales and brand promotion. Need to be available Friday - Sunday 11 am to 7 PM. Extra hours are available during bottling and harvest. $15-$20/hr plus tips.

Vineyard technician is an agricultural position to help manage vines and trellis work through the growing season. Vineyard tech is a part time position 24 hours per week rain or shine. Must have an active interest in vines and vine health in order to maximize yields at harvest. This is a physically demanding job. $15-20/hr based on experience.

For both roles, send resumes to kerry@shootingcreekvines.com
