We're Hiring

We are now seeking applications for tasting room staff and vineyard technician for the 2023 wine season. All applicants must be over 21. Tasting room applicants must be enthusiastic, personable, friendly and capable of sales and brand promotion. Hours are Friday - Sunday 10 am to 6 PM - season runs March - December. Extra hours are available during bottling and harvest. $20/hr plus tips Vineyard technician is an agricultural position to help manage vines and trellis work thru the growing season. This is a physically demanding job. Serious applicants only. $15-20/hr based on experience. Send resumes for consideration to info@shootingcreekvines.com
